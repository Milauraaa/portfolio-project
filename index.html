Hello! Let's get to know each other!
My name is Mila. I have been working in the best restaurants in Vinnytsia and Odesa since I was 17. Starting as a waiter, in a few years I became a professional manager for a team of 50 to 90 people. I always felt I had a tendency towards IT, so even when I was working as a manager, I took responsibility for all digital issues. I like to simplify complex things, find simple solutions and bring creativity and unusualness to work. I'm a little crazy, but I consider it as my advantage, because I know how to evaluate situations from different angles. The war changed all of my priorities and I had to leave my native home, work, goals, dreams. Now I have to start all over again. Because of this difficult step, I found coding for myself and discovered new sides of myself in website development. Probably, for the first time in my life, I feel one hundred percent sure that it is mine. I want to improve and write such codes that will take the breath away from the most demanding project customers!
